Councilwoman Leslie Knope

Councilwoman Leslie Knope was elected in 2012 after serving as the Deputy Director of the Pawnee Parks and Recreation department.

Li'l Sebastian

Li’l Sebastian is Pawnee’s prized mini horse. He’s been all around the world, performing for the troops in Iraq and Kuwait, as well as for the Queen at Buckingham palace and at home for the citizens of Pawnee. Unfortunately, Li’l Sebastian passed away in 2013, however, his legacy continues to live on in the hearts of minds of everyone in Pawnee.

Mouse Rat

A staple of the Pawnee local music scene, rock band “Mouse Rat” performs frequently at shows around town. Mouse Rat’s lead singer and guitarist is none other than the Pawnee Parks and Recreation department’s very own Andy Dwyer.

Sweetums

Sweetums is Pawnee’s crown jewel for business and for candy. The town's sweet tooth residents love the bounty of delicious flavors from all of Sweetums creations.

Miss Pawnee Beauty Pagent

The annual Miss Pawnee Beauty Pagent celebrates the best Pawnee has to offer. Contestants compete in eveningware, talent, and Q&A portions with the winner receiving a $600 gift card to a local sporting goods store.
